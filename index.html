About

Emile Wamsteker is a web developer and photographer based in Los Angeles, CA.

They say necessity is the mother of invention. In 2018, Wamsteker was searching for a way to harvest keyword candidates from text but could not find anything available off-the-shelf. After a little research on the internet, he was introduced to the field of Natural Language Processing, and a computer language called Python. Eventually he learned enough Python to create a keyword harvester. The act of creating something that could be used over and over again, to do heavy lifting, excited him as much as the first time he witnessed the magic of photographic development in the darkroom. In the same way as the proverbial photo bug bites, he was bitten by the code bug.
